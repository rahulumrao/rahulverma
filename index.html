About me

I’m Rahul, working in the filed of Theoretical and Computational Chemistry. Since February 2024, I work as a Postdoc Research Scholar at the Pfaendtner Research Group in the NC State University.

I completed my PhD at the Prof. Nisanth Nair's Lab, where I worked on the applications of QM/MM techniques to model reactions in zeolites.

I care about promoting open, collaborative, and reproducible science. In my free time, you will find me playing badmintion and watching movies.
